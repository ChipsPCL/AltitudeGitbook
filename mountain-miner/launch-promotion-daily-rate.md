# Launch Promotion Daily Rate

Mountain Miner is planned to launch with a promotional daily rate structure.

## Planned Rate Schedule

| Period | Daily Rate |
|---|---:|
| Days 1-30 | 1% daily |
| Days 31-60 | 0.75% daily |
| Day 61 onward | 0.5% daily until end game |

This structure rewards early users while gradually reducing the daily rate as the miner matures.

If users claim efficiently during the early period, the launch structure is designed so that early users may reach ROI around day 158 from launch.

{% hint style="info" %}
Final ROI timing depends on claim behaviour, fees, contract balance, and final contract settings.
{% endhint %}
